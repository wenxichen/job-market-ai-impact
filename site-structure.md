# Neutral site structure

## Home page
- Short intro: this is a public archive of AI/job-market predictions
- No scoring, no verdicts, no editorial ranking
- Search bar
- Filters

## Filters
- speaker
- speaker type
- organization
- date made
- topic tag
- geography
- prediction type
- target year / time horizon

## Prediction card
- prediction text
- speaker name
- role / organization
- date made
- source title
- source link
- tags
- time horizon

## Prediction detail page
- full quote
- source metadata
- related predictions by same speaker
- related predictions on same topic

## Good default copy
"This archive collects public predictions about how AI may affect jobs, wages, productivity, occupations, and labor markets. Entries are presented neutrally and linked to their sources."

## Important editorial rule
Separate:
- what was predicted
- who said it
- when they said it
- where it was said

Do not mix in:
- whether you agree
- whether it came true
- your interpretation

That can be phase 2.
